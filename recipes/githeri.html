Githeri

Preparing a tasty githeri

Githeri is loved by many because its simple to cook ,healthy and tasty.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups beans - boiled
  • 2cups maize-boiled
  • 2 large potatoes peeled and chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes-diced
  • 1 small onion-sliced
  • 3tbs cooking oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 large carrots-chopped
  • 1 avocado

Cooking Steps

  1. Place the onions in a sufuria and add the cooking oil. when it turns a little brown add the tomatoes and stir then cover.
  2. Crash the tomatoes when they become soft. Add the carrots and stir then add the mixed beans and maize and stir.
  3. Add the potatoes and 1 cup of water and 1/2tsp salt and stir then cover for 10 minutes. if the water finishes before the potatoes are cooked add1/2 cup of water and stir then cover for 5 minutes then turn off the heat and serve.
